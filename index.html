How to use
  1. Open in Chrome on Android (with ARCore) or a WebXR-capable headset browser.
  2. Tap Enter AR, point at the floor, then tap the screen to place the launcher.
  3. Adjust angle and velocity, then tap Throw.
  4. Tap Retrieve Ball — or, on a hand-tracking headset, hold a fist for a moment — to reset.
